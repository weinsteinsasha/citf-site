01 — The festival

World-class theatre, on a Mediterranean island.

The Cyprus International Theatre Festival brings performances you’d normally have to fly to a major capital to see — and stages them in Limassol. From John Malkovich and Pilobolus to Fanny Ardant performing Cassandre with a live symphony orchestra in October 2026.

Our programme spans classic drama, dance, opera and contemporary performance — chosen for one thing: work that moves an audience and could only happen live.

02 — Immersive theatre

WHO ARE YOU? — not a show you watch. An experiment you’re part of.

Two strangers sit face to face and meet through a structured set of written questions. No actors, no stage, no fourth wall — just two people, and what happens between them. Some leave knowing themselves a little better; some leave with a new friend.

It premiered in Limassol in June 2026 — three sold-out sessions in a single day. It is the heart of what we’re building next.

03 — Going international

The format that travels. City by city.

We are now licensing WHO ARE YOU? to producers in other cities, so it can run worldwide under local partners — same care, fully adapted to each place and language. Cyprus was the proof of concept; the world is the roadmap.

Partners get a complete kit — the production manual, questionnaires, brand, training and ongoing support — everything needed to stage the experience in their own city.
